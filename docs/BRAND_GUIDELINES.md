# IronTrack Brand Guidelines

## Primary Logo

Use the **IronTrack Project Pulse** lockup for every app demo, website demo, pitch mockup, screenshot, and marketing concept.

The product can be positioned as subcontractor-first, but the visible brand lockup remains:

> IRONTRACK PROJECT PULSE

## Assets

- Light backgrounds: `/irontrack-project-pulse-logo.svg`
- Dark backgrounds: `/irontrack-project-pulse-logo-dark.svg`
- App icon / compact mark: `/irontrack-app-icon.svg`

## Usage Rules

- Use the full Project Pulse lockup in website headers, app/demo headers, marketing mockups, and generated design concepts.
- Use the orange app icon only when space is constrained, such as favicons, small nav icons, or compact mobile affordances.
- Do not replace the logo subtitle with "Subcontractor Pulse" in demos. Keep subcontractor-first positioning in headlines and body copy instead.
- Keep the orange pulse icon, bold IRONTRACK wordmark, and PROJECT PULSE subtitle together when presenting the brand.
- Maintain clear space around the logo and avoid placing it on busy photos without a dark or light backing surface.

## Demo Prompt Reminder

When generating app or website demo images, include the IronTrack Project Pulse logo lockup visibly in the header or opening frame.
